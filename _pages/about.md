## Research

My research lies at the intersection of ubiquitous computing, synthetic data generation, and large language models (LLMs). I build intelligent simulation systems where LLM-guided embodied agents generate virtual sensor data‚Äîsuch as motion, ambient, and wearable signals‚Äîinside realistic environments. This work aims to scale human activity recognition (HAR) beyond the limits of costly, privacy-sensitive real-world data.
